Born on the unique date of 01/01/01, Kris Yang has sought to make the content of his life as unique as his date of birth. It was rather apparent for Kris and those around him that Kris would be going into the Art industry as on top of a passion for drawing, Kris had quickly proven himself to be rather creative. As a result, he was enrolled in Art School from an early age, something that would prove to be very beneficial in the coming years.

By the end of High Schhol, Kris was already skilled in animating and was making short animation online. He was already well skilled in online commerce as he set up his own online merch store where he sold stickers and keychains of popular characters of his shorts. These days, Kris has been experimenting with 3D animation and also plans on expanding into making clothings for his merch.

Despite his schedule of constant animation, Kris is still dedicated to completing his education, taking classes at UCR as an Art Major in order to better enrich his profolio for future employment, most likely in the Art Industry.
